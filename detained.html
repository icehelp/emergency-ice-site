If Someone Is Being Detained

Stop. Breathe. Stay calm.

  • Write down full name and date of birth
  • Ask where they are being taken (if safe)
  • Document time, place, vehicles
  • Call for legal help immediately

Posting bond

  • Bonds are usually paid in full
  • Online payment via ICE CeBONDS is common
  • Some offices allow in-person payment (call ahead)
  • A lawyer can request a bond hearing if denied
