ICE Is at My Door

Stop. Breathe. Stay calm.

If possible, put children and pets in a separate room.

DO NOT OPEN THE DOOR

Do not open the door. Do not let them in.

Say this (out loud)

“I do not consent to you entering my home.
Please show me a warrant signed by a judge.”
“I am exercising my right to remain silent.
I want to speak to a lawyer.”

Important reminders

  • ICE can lie to get you to open the door
  • An ICE administrative warrant is usually not enough
  • Ask to see a judge-signed warrant through a window or under the door
