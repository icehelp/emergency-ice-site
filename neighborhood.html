ICE Is in My Neighborhood

Stop. Breathe. Stay calm.
Seeing ICE nearby does not automatically mean they can enter homes or that you must engage.

Do this first

  • Go inside if you feel unsafe
  • Keep doors locked
  • Do not approach officers
  • Do not spread rumors

If you document: record from a safe distance. Do not interfere physically.

Public accountability

If no one is in immediate danger and it is safe, sharing verified information publicly may increase accountability. Do not live-post if it could escalate the situation or put anyone at risk. Share facts only.
